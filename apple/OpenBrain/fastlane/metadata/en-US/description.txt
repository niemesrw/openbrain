Brain is a personal AI knowledge base that lives in your own AWS cloud. It stores your thoughts, decisions, ideas, and notes as semantic embeddings — so you can search by meaning, not just keywords.

CAPTURE ANYTHING
Quickly save observations, tasks, ideas, references, and person notes. Auto-detect lets the AI classify your thought for you.

SEARCH BY MEANING
Ask natural language questions like "what did I decide about the API design?" and Brain finds the relevant thought — even if you used completely different words when you wrote it.

BROWSE YOUR MEMORY
Scroll through recent thoughts filtered by type. See what's been on your mind.

CHAT WITH YOUR BRAIN
Have a conversation with your knowledge base. Ask follow-up questions, get summaries, and explore connections between your thoughts.

YOUR DATA, YOUR CLOUD
Brain connects to your own Open Brain backend on AWS. Your thoughts are stored in your own S3 Vectors index — not on our servers. Sign in with Google to get started.

PRIVACY FIRST
No ads. No data sharing. No third-party analytics. Everything lives in your own AWS account.
